LCM (Least Common Multiple)

  • Dui ba tatodhik sankhyar smallest common multiple
  • Method: Prime factorization ba Division method
  • LCM × HCF = Product of two numbers
Quick Formula
Two numbers a, b:
LCM × HCF = a × b

HCF (Highest Common Factor)

  • Dui ba tatodhik sankhyar largest common factor
  • Method: Prime factorization ba Euclid Division
  • HCF always ≤ smaller number
💡HCF of co-prime numbers = 1. LCM of co-prime = their product.

Fraction Operations

  • Addition/Subtraction: Same denominator banao, tarpor numerator add/sub koro

  • Division: Second fraction flip kore multiply koro (reciprocal)
  • Decimal → Fraction: Denominator = 10, 100, 1000 (decimal places anujayee)

Mathematics Pedagogy ★★★

Exam e sure questions — theory bujhle easy

Bruner er 3 Stage Theory

  • Enactive Stage — Hands-on, actual objects diye shikhano (concrete)
  • Iconic Stage — Pictures o diagrams diye represent kora
  • Symbolic Stage — Abstract symbols o numbers use kora
💡Primary shishu ke concrete theke abstract e niye jao — Bruner er principle

Dienes er Principles (Math Learning)

  • Dynamic Principle — Khele khele sheko (manipulatives)
  • Constructivity Principle — Nijer haathe banao
  • Mathematical Variability — Same concept, alag alag way e
  • Perceptual Variability — Same structure, different materials e

Polya er Problem Solving Steps

  • Step 1: Understand the problem — ki janate chaicha?
  • Step 2: Devise a plan — kemon kore solve korbo?
  • Step 3: Carry out the plan — plan execute koro
  • Step 4: Look back — answer check koro
💡Polya er book: "How to Solve It" (1945)

Common Math Errors in Children

  • Place value bujhte problem (23 ke "twenty three" na bujha)
  • Fraction e numerator o denominator alag alag add kora
  • Subtraction e borrow kora bujhte problem
  • 0 ke divisor hisebe use kora
  • Negative number concept na bujha
