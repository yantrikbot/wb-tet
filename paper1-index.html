🎯 Top Exam Tips

🧠

CDP te aro beshi focus koro

Piaget, Vygotsky, Kohlberg theory gulo theke ek question sure ashe. Theory er names o theorists gulo mone rakho.

📖

Comprehension skip koro na

Language I & II te passage theke sure marks. Passage ta age poro, tarpor question — aro fast hoy.

🔢

Math e concept bujhe nao

Rote noy, concept bujhle ek question theke onek variation solve kora jay. LCM, HCF, fraction practice koro.

🌿

EVS e NCERT follow koro

NCERT Class 3, 4, 5 er EVS theke direct questions ashe. Themes-based study koro — Food, Water, Travel.

⏭️

Stuck hole skip koro

Ek question e 2 minuter beshi noy. Mark kore porer question e jao — sheshe time thakle phiro esho.

✅

Kono question na chere esho

Negative marking nei! Sob question e answer koro — guess o korle 25% chance ache thike jawar.
